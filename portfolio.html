Data Engineering

Scaling Data Products with Metadata-Driven Pipelines

Enabled scalable, higher-quality data product delivery across highly variable source datasets.

Databricks Spark Data quality
Read the story

As part of my work at Kenway Consulting, I partnered with a client launching new data products from complex and inconsistent datasets. The existing approach required significant custom engineering for each new source, which limited scale.

The challenge

Source data varied widely in structure, metadata, and format. Without a reusable approach, each new data product added complexity and slowed delivery timelines.

What I did

  • Designed a metadata-driven pipeline architecture in Databricks
  • Established configuration-based processing for onboarding new sources
  • Standardized transformations across a medallion-style architecture
  • Embedded validation, profiling, and anomaly detection into pipelines

Outcome

  • Shifted source onboarding from custom builds to configuration-driven updates
  • Improved trust in downstream data products through integrated quality checks
  • Supported scalable delivery without increasing engineering overhead
